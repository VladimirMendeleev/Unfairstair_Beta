About

In a vertical dungeon, a brave knight and in pursuit of a terrible black mage! Indeed he cast a curse and stole all the gold from the villagers of the city of unfair city. Fleeing up his dilapidated tower! The knight will have to carefully climb the thousands of steps of the "unfair stair". On his way, the mage sowed gold coins from his previous larceny. Your role is to collect them. However, time is against you.

Will you arrive in time to stop the dark mage?
